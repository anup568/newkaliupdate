Washington Kali Temple Seva Form

As we usher in another year of divine blessings at the Washington Kali Temple, we take a moment to express our heartfelt gratitude for the unwavering support of our dedicated volunteers. The temple thrives on the selfless service of individuals like you, who contribute their time and skills to create a sacred and vibrant community.

Diverse Seva Opportunities Await You!

Our temple community flourishes through volunteer contributions. From registration desk duties to marketing initiatives, your unique skills can make a profound impact. Express your interest in various areas and share your availability. Let us together streamline processes, automate tasks, and elevate the overall temple experience.

Calling All Writers and Marketers!

Dive into creative realms by contributing to our weekly newsletter and marketing endeavors. If you possess the gift of crafting compelling content, join us in sharing divine stories and temple updates. Sign up for content writing, marketing roles, or both, and help us amplify our temple’s digital presence.

Efficient Seva Management – Your Suggestions Welcome!

Excitingly, we’re implementing a quarterly automated seva scheduling system. Your insights on optimizing this process are highly valued. Share your suggestions for enhancing volunteer activities and creating a seamless experience. Together, let’s fortify the spirit of seva and fortify our temple community.

To express your interest and contribute to these divine activities, kindly provide your details, preferred areas of involvement, and availability. Additionally, secure your slot for bhog cooking—seva slots will be allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Your dedication remains the cornerstone of our vibrant Washington Kali Temple community.

Reach Out to Washington Kali Temple

Have questions about temple services, pujas, or upcoming events? We’re here to help. Feel free to reach out to us for any inquiries, donations, or assistance. We welcome your visit to the sacred abode of Maa Kali.

Since the founding in March of 2002, The Washington Kali Temple has made great strides in its mission to build a facility for a complete spiritual and religious experience, to worship Goddess Kali, Goddess Durga, Lord Shiva and Ganesh, and Radha Krishna as well as Nabagrahas. This temple is a shining example of what a community could accomplish collectively in order to sustain its religious and cultural roots.
